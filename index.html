About UT-STAR

The University of Toronto Space Technology and Research (UT-STAR) Club is a student-led organization dedicated to advancing space science and technology. We bring together passionate students from diverse academic backgrounds to work on cutting-edge projects, participate in competitions, and explore the frontiers of space exploration.
