About Us

Hi there! I am the President and Founder of UofT STAR. As a second‑year math and physics double major with a strong interest in rocketry, I created this team to bring together students passionate about aerospace. We’re proud to be the only rocketry team run by Faculty of Arts & Science students, aiming to become the go‑to rocketry group for FAS and beyond.

Our mission is to design, test, and launch rockets while giving students valuable experience in CAD, propulsion, fluid dynamics, avionics, and systems engineering. We welcome students of all backgrounds and experience levels — join us to learn, build, and fly!

Projects

VTOL Demonstrator

A vertical take‑off and landing rocket testbed for thrust vector control, guidance, and navigation.

Avionics

Flight computers, telemetry, and ground control software using C/C++ and Python with simulation testing.

Structures & Propulsion

Airframe design, composite structures, motor integration, and thrust stand testing.

Our Team

We’re a diverse group of students spanning physics, engineering, computer science, and more. Our executive committee of eight leads subsystems including avionics, propulsion, and structures.
